How it works

Simple from the start.

Four steps from first conversation to settlement. Then we stay in your corner.

1
Start with a conversation
We talk through your situation, goals, and what you want from your loan. No forms, no jargon — just a genuine conversation about where you're at and where you want to be.
2
We search the market for you
We compare 40+ lenders — not just on rate, but on policy, turnaround time, and structure. Then we explain your options clearly and recommend the right fit for your situation.
3
We handle everything
Paperwork, lender communication, solicitor coordination — all managed by us. Your job is to find the right home. Ours is to make sure the finance is sorted.
4
Stay in touch
After settlement we monitor your loan, check in every 6 months, and reach out when market conditions or your circumstances change. Your loan should keep working for you.
