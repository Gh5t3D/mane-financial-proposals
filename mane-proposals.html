Below are three distinct design approaches for the Mane Financial website. Each uses different typography, colour systems, and layout logic. Click Open in full on any option to review it at full scale.

Option A
Option A
Midnight Editorial
Charcoal · Amber · Bebas Neue + Libre Baskerville

Bold and unapologetic. Deep charcoal backgrounds with electric amber accents. Large Bebas Neue display type sits beside italic Baskerville serif — a confident contrast that feels more like a financial magazine than a mortgage broker's website. Services are a hover-animated horizontal list. The amber ticker band below the hero adds movement.

Option B
Option B
Soft Prestige
Warm White · Terracotta · DM Serif Display + Epilogue

Refined and approachable. Pure white and warm off-white with a single terracotta accent — no competing colours, no noise. DM Serif Display in italic cuts handles all headlines. Service rows use a subtle vertical bar that animates up on hover. The calculator sits inside the process section for a natural flow. Feels elevated without being cold.

Option C
Option C
Warm Authority
Forest Green · Cream · Copper · Cormorant Garamond

Premium and grounded. Deep forest green on the hero right panel contrasts with warm cream throughout. Cormorant Garamond brings an authoritative, almost legal-document quality to headlines. The rolling copper marquee strip below the hero adds character. Services use a clean two-column grid with copper underline reveals. Best suited to clients who want to project experience and trustworthiness over everything else.
